emma butterworth
emma butterworth is a graphic designer, videographer, illustrator, painter, muralist, amateur filmmaker, image maker, wannabe wordsmith + comedien, and just generally a creative-type individual who lives and works in richmond, virginia. alongside her constant exploration of these various mediums, emma is full-time student in virginia commonwealth university's graphic design program and does freelance design, illustration, and videography for "read to them", a children's literacy nonprofit. informing her work is a desire to make immersive, emotional, impactful design and films. emma wants to produce worldview-altering works that take people's breath away--or at the very least linger with them for a moment. she don't take herself too seriously, and neither does her work, despite caring enormously. overall, emma would like to make work that actually does something. feel free to reach out if you're interested in emma's design, videography, or painting services.
